iPhone 17 Review:
A Touch of Perfection

The iPhone 17 is a testament to Apple's relentless pursuit of excellence. With its sleek design, powerful performance, and innovative features, it sets a new standard in the smartphone industry. The device boasts a stunning OLED display that offers vibrant colors and deep blacks, making every visual experience.

Photo Taken by Georgiy Lyamin

The iPhone 17 coming in at a price of RM3,999, features an A19 processor, while the three other handsets, which are the iPhone Air, iPhone 17 Pro and iPhone 17 Pro Max offer the more powerful A19 pro, but coming in at a more expensive price. The cameras have also had a boost across the range, with a new front-facing 18MP Center Stage camera for selfies and video calls.

   All the iPhones for 2025 offer Always-On and ProMotion displays, so 120Hz refresh rates are no longer the domain of the Pro models only. The new iPhone 17 lineup also includes significant internal and external changes and improved battery life across the range.

Photo Taken by Brad Pouncey

Overall, the iPhone 17 is a remarkable device that combines style, functionality, and cutting-edge technology. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a casual user, the iPhone 17 is sure to impress with its exceptional performance and user-friendly features.
